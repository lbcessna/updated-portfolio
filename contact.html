Hello, and welcome to my page!


I grew up on the Florida panhandle but I have called the beautiful Pacific Northwest my home for most of my adult life.


My professional life has taken me around the world. Literally! I spent 20 years of my life in the US Navy departing in 2015 at the rank of Chief Petty Officer.


I wanted to change gears and focus on IT. Upon my departure from Active Duty, I worked as an Incident Manager on Microsoft's Escalation Management team. From there I completed my Master of Science in Information/Project Management and began working as a Technincal Project Manager for UnitedHealth Group.


Now I'm pivoting to Web Development. Becoming a developer has been a dream of mine for a few years. So, I took the leap and applied for University of Washington's Full Stack Javascript bootcamp, which will be completed in November 2020.

Feel free to look around at some of the projects that I'm proud of on Github. Or even better, reach out and connect with me on LinkedIn!
